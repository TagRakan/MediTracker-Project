Features

Everything you need to stay on track

From smart dose scheduling to family health management, MediTracker covers every aspect of your medication journey.

💊

Smart Dose Scheduling

Set flexible weekly schedules with custom times, amounts, and start/end dates.

📅

Daily Schedule View

Browse any day on a scrollable calendar. See pending, taken, skipped, and missed doses at a glance with full history.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Center

Add family members with relationship types and monitor their active doses and history.

🩺

Doctor Connect

Doctors can prescribe doses directly to patients using a secure 6-character code system. Full patient management portal.

🌐

Arabic & English

Full RTL support for Arabic with instant language switching. Every screen adapts layout direction automatically.

🏥

Ailment Tracking

Track health conditions with status (ongoing, recovering, cured, chronic). Doctors can view and update patient ailments.

About the project

Built by students, for everyone

MediTracker is a full-stack graduation project developed at Hail University. It combines a React Native mobile app with a Spring Boot backend to deliver a complete medication management platform.

The app supports patients, doctors, and admins with role-based access, family health monitoring, and a fully localized Arabic/English interface.
